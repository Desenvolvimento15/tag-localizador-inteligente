Enhanced Features Smarter. Simpler. More Intuitive
How it works? Xiaomi Tag sends out a secure Bluetooth signal that can be detected by nearby devices. These devices anonymously upload the location to the cloud, allowing you to see it in the relevant tracking app. The entire process is encrypted to protect your privacy, and the system is highly efficient, so you don't need to worry about battery life or data usage.8 8.Xiaomi Tag transmits signals via Bluetooth, and its detection and tracking rely on third-party "Find My Device" network support. This signal can only be recognized by devices connected to the corresponding network. The Apple Find My network and the Google Android Find Hub network operate independently; therefore, cross-network detection or location tracking is not supported.
Share with others Easily share Xiaomi Tag with your family and friends, so they can help you find lost items.
Left-behind alerts When an item is separated from you and is no longer in a frequent location, the app will send a notification.9 Pre-set your frequent locations to avoid unnecessary alerts when your item is in a familiar place.10 9.Left-behind alerts are supported only on Apple Find My. 10.You can add up to 20 frequent locations.
Lost mode You can mark an item as lost and add the owner's contact details along with a message. If someone finds the item, they can access the owner's contact information. Google Find Hub users: When a Xiaomi Tag is detected nearby, a pop-up will appear with the owner's details. Apple Find My users: Tap the Xiaomi Tag with an NFC-enabled smartphone to view the owner's details.
Privacy protection Safe and reliable Xiaomi Tag location data is encrypted during transmission, ensuring only you can see its location. If an unknown Tag follows you for an extended period, the system will send an anti-tracking alert.11 11.Anti-tracking support varies by Find network. Please refer to the official Apple Find My and Google Find Hub websites for detailed support information.
Long-lasting battery >1 year Overall battery life
The Tag uses standard, easily replaceable batteries that provide up to one year of battery life. The app also sends timely low-battery alerts, ensuring convenient and worry-free use.5,6

IP67 Dust and water resistance Resistant to sun, rain, and dust, Xiaomi Tag can withstand everyday bumps and minor accidents.7 7.The device has been tested and certified to be resistant to splash, water, and dust in the presence of specific laboratory conditions with the classification IP67 ingress protection based on IEC 60529:1989+A1:1999+A2:2013. Please note that the test conditions of water resistance include: submersion in static freshwater up to a depth of 1 meter, up to 30 minutes, with a temperature delta between the water and product being 5K or less. Such water resistance features only pertain to specific conditions tested in a laboratory environment, which do not correspond to the normal conditions of use by consumers. Therefore, ingress protection is not assured if the product is subjected to environments beyond the test conditions. We recommend that you do not test it by yourself. Not advised for beach or pool use. Ingress protection might deteriorate from daily wear and tear, physical damage, and/or disassembly needed in case of repair. Avoid changing the battery or operating the physical button when the product is exposed to moisture. Please carefully refer to the user's manual for other safety instructions. The warranty will not cover the liquid damage caused in the environments beyond the IP67 test condition.
